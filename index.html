PHD Engineering

Here at PHD Engineering we specialize in process improvements for the constant pursuit of quality improvements and cost reduction. We design and build all kinds of custom assembly fixtures. These fixtures include part conformation fixtures, press assembly, electrical probe test fixtures, data collection measurement and SPC gauges, process automation systems and controls, ultrasonic horns and nests, spot welding heads and fixtures, custom work stations for ergonomic assembly work and safety controls, machine guarding and interlocks.
